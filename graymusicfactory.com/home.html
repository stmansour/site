THE HITS YOU NEVER HEARD
GRAY is a new group of ageless musician/writers whose youthful dreams and record industry contacts were exchanged for the joys of love and children. Tethered to this joy was the responsibility of providing for our families. Now, 30 years later and after our recent escape from the corporate world we reassert our quest for fame and chicks (albeit, rather more mature chicks) and now fueled by an obsession to share the stories – our stories – of life, love and an uncertain society.
